Our Story

A groomer who actually cares

"It's not just grooming — it's creating a positive experience that both dogs and their owners can truly feel good about."

Hi, I'm Jillianne Irizarry — owner and lead groomer at Jill's Pet Spaw in Freeport, FL. I started this business because I believed dogs deserved better than the rushed, high-volume experience most groomers offer. Five years later, that belief is still at the core of everything I do.

I groom one dog at a time. Your pet has my full, undivided attention from the moment they arrive to the moment you pick them up. I don't rush. I take my time to ensure each dog feels safe, comfortable, and genuinely cared for from start to finish.

My specialty is creating a low-stress experience while delivering high-quality, detail-obsessed grooming. I focus on the things that often get overlooked — proper coat prep, gentle handling, and truly customizing every groom to the individual dog.

Patience Over Speed

I never rush a groom. Every dog gets the time they need to feel safe.

Genuine Care

Building trust with dogs and their families is what makes this work fulfilling.
